Using that $\exp \diag (a_j) = \diag(e^{a_j})$ we could simplify
\begin{equation*}
	e^{- i \theta \hat{\sigma}_z} \hat{\sigma}_x e^{i \theta \hat{\sigma}_z} = 
	\begin{pmatrix}
	    e^{-i \theta} & 0 \\
	    0 & e^{i  \theta} \\
	\end{pmatrix} \begin{pmatrix}
	    0 & 1 \\
	    1 & 0 \\
	\end{pmatrix}
	\begin{pmatrix}
	    e^{i \theta} & 0 \\
	    0 & e^{-i  \theta} \\
	\end{pmatrix}
	=
	\cos(2\theta) \hat{\sigma}_x + \sin(2\theta) \hat{\sigma}_y.
\end{equation*}
