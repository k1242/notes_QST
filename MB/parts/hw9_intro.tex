Рассмотрим one-dimensional Fermi-Hubbard model with 
\begin{equation*}
	\hat{H} = 
	- J \sum_{\langle i,j\rangle} (\hat{a}_i\D \hat{a}_j + \hc) 
	+ \Delta \sum_j \varepsilon_j \hat{n}_j

\end{equation*}
